Rules

Server & Trade Rules

Read these carefully before opening a ticket. By using SwapSafe, you agree to all rules below. Violations may result in a ban and loss of protection.

1. General Conduct

  • • Be respectful. No harassment, hate speech, or threats.
  • • No advertising of other servers or services without permission.
  • • Keep discussions in the appropriate channels.

2. Trading

  • • All trades must go through an official SwapSafe middleman ticket.
  • • Both parties must clearly state and agree to terms in the ticket.
  • • Never release items or funds outside of escrow.
  • • Screenshots or logs from outside the ticket will not be honored.

3. Verifying Middlemen

  • • Official staff have the @Staff role and are listed in #staff.
  • • Staff will never DM you first. Any DM claiming to be staff is a scam.
  • • Always verify the Discord ID, not just the username.

4. Fees

Trade ValueFee
Under $100Free
$100 – $1,0002%
$1,000 – $10,0001.5%
Over $10,000Contact staff

Fee is split 50/50 between buyer and seller by default.

5. Disputes

  • • If something goes wrong, do not leave the ticket. Click Ask for support.
  • • Funds will remain in escrow until the dispute is resolved.
  • • Provide evidence inside the ticket - admin decisions are final.

6. Prohibited Trades

  • Stolen accounts, credentials, or assets
  • Illegal goods or services
  • Anything involving minors
  • Chargeback-prone payment methods without prior approval

Questions about the rules?

Ask staff in #support before starting a trade.
