How SwapSafe Escrow Works

A step-by-step walkthrough of what happens from the moment you open a ticket to the moment the trade is finalized.

1

Open a Ticket

Head to #open-ticket in our Discord and click the button to create a private escrow channel. Only you, the other party, and staff will see it.

2

Agree on Terms

Both parties clearly state what is being traded, the price, and any special conditions. A middleman will review and lock the agreed terms.

3

Buyer Funds Escrow

The buyer sends funds (crypto or fiat) to the escrow address provided by the middleman. Funds are held securely and cannot be touched by the seller yet.

4

Seller Delivers

Once escrow is confirmed by the middleman, the seller delivers the item, account, or service. Everything is logged in the ticket.

5

Buyer Confirms & Release

The buyer confirms receipt. The middleman then releases the funds to the seller and the ticket is archived for record-keeping.
